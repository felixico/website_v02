Felixico

We believe that anyone can dance if the music being played flows through one's veins.

We started out DJing for friend's birthday party's and private events, separately might I add until we saw the potential to combine our talents and become a duo.

The first event we DJ'd was a huge back to school party for teachers and their friends in the summer of 2013. Held at a house that was clearly over its max capacity 3 times over and it was in the summer so it felt like a sweat box in there. People didn't even mind, they just kept dancing and with the success of that we knew the type of music we played and the way we engaged with the crowd could mean huge potential for success if we really took this seriously.

Next thing you know we had booked our first wedding to DJ together that next January and from there even picked up a gig with the Arizona Diamondbacks and since then we've kept moving forward keeping people dancing along the way.

Services

Weddings

We have DJ'd a wide variety of weddings and pride ourselves at being great communicators throughout the duration of the planning phase and throughout the wedding itself. We also MC if needed.

Church Events

Church events are always filled with such great community. What better way to boost the atmosphere then to have DJ's.

Sporting Events

Having already DJ'd a season for the Arizona Diamondbacks we are experienced in creating a vibe for sports fans. Keeping fans entertained and happy was something that we excelled at doing everytime.

Graduation Parties

Graduating from school is always a reason to celebrate and we are always in tune with the latest and hottest tracks out there that will guarantee a graduation party you'll never forget.

Corporate Events

Multi-cultural events

Holiday Parties
